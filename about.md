I am a Ph.D student working in Artificial Intelligence.

I am especially interested in the mathematical structures that appear across machine learning and related areas.

This blog is my personal study notebook. I use it to organize ideas that I encounter while reading textbooks, papers, and research notes. The goal is not to present polished or final knowledge, but to translate difficult concepts into my own language.

One recurring theme in my study is the intuitive and physical interpretation of mathematical expressions. Many equations in machine learning and AI are not merely formal objects; they tell dynamic stories! I want to understand these ideas not only symbolically, but also conceptually.
